How do enhanced for loop and index-based for loop differ?
- The former provides copy of value and the latter provides position of value, so you can't use enhanced for loop to modify a `Iterable` object by assigning.
How do **formula** and **equation** differ?
- A **formula** is a specific type of equation that expresses a known, proven relationship between variables, while an **equation** is a more general statement of equality that may or may not be true and is often meant to be solved.
